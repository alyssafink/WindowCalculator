Thermal Comfort

Windows play a huge role in how comfortable your home is. While your HVAC systems counteract the unwanted heat let into and out of your home, the biggest impact on comfort from windows comes from the temperature of the glass itself. This is because the temperature your body feels depends on both the air temperature and the temperature of surfaces around you, like a brick wall in the sun. The upgrades recommended by the Window Calculator - low-e window film, low-e storm windows, and ENERGY STAR replacement windows - all work to control the temperature of your windows during extreme heat, keeping you cooler in the summer and warmer in the winter.
The Window Calculator estimates how much warmer than the thermostat set point your home could feel on a typical summer afternoon based on the information you shared about your home.
